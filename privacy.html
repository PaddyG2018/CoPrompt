CoPrompt V2 Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Introduction

CoPrompt V2 is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our Chrome extension with our credit-based service.

Data Collection

We collect the following types of data:

  • Your email address for user authentication
  • Your OpenAI API key (encrypted and stored securely on our servers)
  • Credit balance and usage tracking
  • Conversation context from supported AI platforms (sent temporarily for enhancement)
  • Usage statistics and error logs
  • Button position preferences (stored locally)

Data Usage

Your data is used to:

  • Authenticate your account and manage your credit balance
  • Enhance your prompts using the OpenAI API
  • Track credit usage and prevent abuse
  • Improve the extension's functionality
  • Debug and fix issues
  • Remember your preferences

Data Protection

We implement several security measures to protect your data:

  • API keys are encrypted using industry-standard encryption before server storage
  • User authentication is handled through secure Supabase Auth
  • All server communications use HTTPS encryption
  • Row-level security policies protect your data from unauthorized access
  • Regular security audits and updates

Data Retention

Your data is retained as follows:

  • Account information: Until you delete your account
  • API keys: Until you remove them or delete your account
  • Credit usage logs: 1 year for billing and analytics
  • Local preferences: Until you reset or uninstall the extension
